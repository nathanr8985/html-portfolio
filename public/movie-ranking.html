The Best Movies According to Nathan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bridesmades

This is my all-time favorite situational comedies. The hilarious situations the main characters find themselves in keep the laughs rolling through the credits.

October Sky

This movie is based on true events surrounding the space race and offers a heartwarming glimps into the main characters' passion for science and patirotism.

Superman: Man of Steele

This live-action take on the superhero classic that is the Superman universe was beautifully scripted and the cinematography is outstanding.
